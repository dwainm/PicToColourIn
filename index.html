Technical Details

Our advanced WebGL pipeline uses Gaussian blur for noise reduction, grayscale conversion, Sobel edge detection for finding edges, and intelligent thresholding to create clean black-and-white line art perfect for coloring.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is PicToColourIn free to use?

Yes, PicToColourIn is completely free to use. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or watermarks. Convert as many photos to coloring pages as you like.

Is my photo data safe and private?

Absolutely. All image processing happens directly in your browser using WebGL and GPU acceleration. Your photos never leave your device or get uploaded to any server. We have zero access to your images.

What image formats are supported?

PicToColourIn supports all common image formats including JPG, PNG, WebP, and GIF. The maximum file size depends on your device's memory, but most photos work perfectly.

Can I use this on my phone or tablet?

Yes! PicToColourIn works on all devices including iPhones, iPads, Android phones and tablets, as well as desktop computers. The interface automatically adapts to your screen size.

How do I get the best coloring page results?

For best results, use photos with clear subjects and good contrast. Portraits and photos with defined edges work best. Use the smoothing slider to reduce noise, adjust edge sensitivity for more or less detail, and set the threshold to control line thickness.

Can I use the coloring pages commercially?

The coloring pages you create are derived from your own photos, so usage rights depend on the original photo's copyright. If you own the photo or have rights to use it, you can use the resulting coloring page as you wish.
